Where do people stay longer in Andorra?

This visualization represents the behavioral patterns of thousands of French, Spanish and other visitors and tourists in Andorra La Vella, during a single day in 2016. Each dot represents a telecom data point aggregated from millions of events. Then, an algorithm tested each individual for duration of 'stay', with an interval of 20 minutes. Each stay event is represented with a vertical line and user's movement with a diagonal one.
